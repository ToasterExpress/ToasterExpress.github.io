Supplement V: Big Bang Comics

The fifth and final supplement for 1st edition was officially endorsed by Gary Carlson and Chris Ecker of Big Bang Comics and was made with their research assistance. It contains new rules for emulating Big Bang Comics, including rules for a time-hopping campaign; new races, mobsters, and trophies culled from the pages of Big Bang Comics; sample stats for the biggest names in the Big Bang Comics universe, and a chronological index of stories from 1938 up into the 1970s.

While not 100% compatible with second edition, Supplement V is a valuable resource for any fan of Big Bang Comics! (This product is only available as an e-book.)
