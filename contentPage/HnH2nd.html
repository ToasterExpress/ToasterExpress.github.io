Hideouts & Hoodlums [Basic Rules] 2nd Edition

This version of H&H (available in both e-book and print) combines the game of 1977 with the comic books of 1935-1941 to create something both comfortably familiar and startlingly new. The game is still largely compatible with first edition, with additional material like a skill mechanic, a new sample hideout, and a sample metropolis to use as a home base for your heroes. This "basic" book contains everything you need to play a endless standalone sessions, or to run a campaign up through sixth level.

This edition was thoroughly researched by reading every comic book readily accessible from the years 1935-1939, so as to best emulate the wild and wacky fun of the comics of those times!
